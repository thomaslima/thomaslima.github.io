Welcome to my personal page.

I am a researcher at NEC Laboratories America. In 2021, I completed my Ph.D. in Paul Prucnal's lab at Princeton University. My research is on Integrated Neuromorphic Photonics, which involves building large-scale photonic circuits on chip that emulate the functionality of neural networks, but working at GHz speed. See my online CV below for more detail.

Recently, all my optoelectronic layout and testing procedures have been solely based on open-source software, of which I am an avid supporter. I believe these tools are superior to commercial-grade software, but they require a steep learning curve. I am committed to making them more user-friendly and mentoring developers and educating users in the community.
